Mobirise

Young band of electronic music from Liege with Onyx to the keyboard and Sam to drums.

Onyx made his first weapons as a DJ before he wanted to propose his own creation. Evolving in the industrial-dark electro universes he wanted to set up a project reflecting his tastes but proposing a musical universe different from the scene he loved so much. This is how he invited Sam, his brother, drummer from the metal hardcore scene, to join the project created in collaboration with Dreadfool : Anamorphosis.

After many years of creation and collaboration, the project splited. Onyx and Sam, the two brothers, naturally stayed together to give birth to a new musical idea : Deleritas! Inspired by their passion for genre cinema, video games and their sensitivity to the news, the brothers compose tracks sometimes black, sometimes striking but always echoing their feelings of the moment. They try different genre sometimes taking inspiration from techno, from EBM and aggrotech but still trying to put their own claw.

Finally, Deleritas is an instrumental project, rich in complicity of the two brothers, expressing themselves most naturally in the world with their machines.
